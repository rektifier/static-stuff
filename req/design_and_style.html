REQ-101 SSO Integration with Azure AD
Version: v1.0
Status: Approved
Priority: High
Owner: Anna Lindstrom
Area: User Authentication
The system shall support single sign-on authentication via Azure Active Directory for all internal users, allowing seamless access without additional credential input. External partner accounts shall be managed through a federated identity model, supporting both OAuth 2.0 and SAML 2.0 protocols. Session management shall comply with the organization's security policy, including automatic timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity.
